# Camping-Checklist
Things to prepare and pack for Camping

Just some basic tips and guidelines

---

## ENGLISH

### Sleeping

* tent
* camping mat / sleeping pad
* sleeping bag
* blanket to put in the anteroom of your tent
* pillow

### Tools

* torch / flashlight
* batteries
* pocket knife / leatherman
* rain shield
* rubber boots
* sunscreen
* sunglasses
* sun hat
* insect protection
* toilet paper
* kitchen roll
* chewing gum
* tissues
* cleaning tissues
* plastic shoes or flipflops
* rubbish bag
* cable drum
* power distributor
* lights or flags to identify your tent
* card games
* fan

### Food/Drink

* camping plates, cups, cutlery
* water can (5L)
* refrigerator box
* camping stove

### Medication

* band-aid
